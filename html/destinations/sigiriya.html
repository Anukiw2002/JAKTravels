Discover Sigiriya



Welcome to Sigiriya, the magnificent 'Lion Rock' fortress that rises dramatically 200 meters above the surrounding plains. This 5th-century UNESCO World Heritage site stands as a testament to ancient Sri Lankan ingenuity, combining a royal palace, sophisticated urban planning, and stunning artistry into one awe-inspiring monument.


Experience this wonder with our expert guides:


  • Early morning climb to avoid crowds and heat
  • Expert narration of the site's rich history and engineering marvels
  • Best photo opportunities at each level of the climb
  • Cultural insights into ancient Sri Lankan civilization
  • Safe navigation of the ancient stairways and passages

Must-Visit Attractions

Explore the wonders of this ancient palace-fortress

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

The crown jewel of ancient Sri Lanka, this stunning palace-fortress includes magnificent water gardens, vibrant frescoes, the mirror wall with ancient graffiti, massive lion paw sculptures, and the summit palace complex. The climb, while challenging, offers increasingly spectacular views and a chance to explore various levels of this architectural marvel. Visit early morning for cooler temperatures and the best lighting for photography.

Pidurangala Rock

This lesser-known but equally impressive rock formation offers the best views of Sigiriya fortress. A more adventurous climb leads to an ancient cave temple and stunning panoramic views. Visit for sunrise or sunset for spectacular photo opportunities of Sigiriya Rock and the surrounding landscape. The climb is more challenging but less crowded than Sigiriya.

Sigiriya Museum

Begin your exploration at this modern museum featuring detailed models, artifacts, and interactive displays that bring the history of Sigiriya to life. Learn about the construction techniques, see artifacts discovered at the site, and understand the historical context of this remarkable fortress through multimedia presentations and archaeological findings.
