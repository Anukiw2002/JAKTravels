Discover Ella



Welcome to Ella, a breathtaking mountain paradise nestled in the heart of Sri Lanka's Hill Country. Let us guide you through this enchanting destination where every corner tells a story and every view captures your heart.


Our expertly crafted tours include:


  • Scenic train journeys through tea plantations
  • Guided hikes to spectacular viewpoints
  • Photography sessions at the iconic Nine Arch Bridge
  • Authentic local experiences and cuisine
  • Comfortable accommodations with mountain views

Must-Visit Destinations

Explore the most scenic locations in Ella with our expert guides

Nine Arch Bridge

Marvel at this architectural masterpiece spanning 91 meters across lush tea plantations. Our guides know the perfect spots and times for photographs.

Ella Rock

Experience an unforgettable sunrise hike with our experienced guides. Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the Ella Gap and surrounding mountains.

Little Adam's Peak

Take an accessible hike to stunning viewpoints. Perfect for both sunrise and sunset photography, with professional guidance from our team.

Ravana Cave

A mysterious cave tied to the Ramayana legend, perfect for history enthusiasts.

Ravana Falls

Visit this majestic waterfall with its rich mythology. Our guides will share ancient legends while you capture perfect photos.

Ella Gap

A breathtaking view through a gap in the hills, offering stunning vistas of the lowlands. Best at sunrise or sunset.
