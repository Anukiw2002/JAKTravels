Discover Galle



Welcome to Galle, a UNESCO World Heritage site that perfectly blends colonial architecture with tropical beauty. This historic coastal city, with its iconic 17th-century Dutch fort, offers a unique journey through time while providing modern comfort and charm.


Our carefully curated tours include:


  • Guided walks along the historic ramparts
  • Exploration of Dutch colonial architecture
  • Visits to historic churches and museums
  • Local craft and gem shopping experiences
  • Luxury stays in restored colonial buildings

Must-Visit Destinations

Explore the most iconic locations in Galle with our expert guides

Galle Fort Ramparts

Walk along the historic 17th-century ramparts for stunning ocean views and sunset vistas. Our guides share fascinating stories of the fort's maritime history and colonial past.

Dutch Reformed Church

Visit this remarkable 1755 church with its historic organ, ornate graves, and beautiful Dutch colonial architecture. Experience one of the oldest Protestant churches in Sri Lanka.

Maritime Museum

Housed in a restored Dutch warehouse, discover Sri Lanka's rich maritime history through artifacts, maps, and naval craft dating back to the colonial era.

Unawatuna Beach

Relax at this crescent-shaped golden beach, perfect for swimming and snorkeling. Enjoy fresh seafood at beachside restaurants and stunning sunset views.

National Museum of Galle

Explore traditional Sri Lankan artifacts, Dutch colonial items, and archaeological treasures in this beautifully preserved building from the Dutch period.

Galle Lighthouse

Visit Sri Lanka's oldest light station still in use, built in 1939. The perfect spot for photographs with its striking white structure against the blue ocean.
