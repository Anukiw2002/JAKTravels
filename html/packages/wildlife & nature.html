Day by Day Itinerary

Your journey through Sri Lanka's wilderness, carefully planned for unforgettable encounters

01
Negombo
Morning

AIRPORT PICKUP

Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, we'll transfer you to your comfortable hotel in Negombo where you can unwind and prepare for the adventure ahead.

Afternoon

FISHING VILLAGE

Visit the vibrant local fishing village, getting your first taste of Sri Lankan coastal life and culture.

02
Sinharaja Rainforest
Morning

JOURNEY TO SINHARAJA

Depart for the magnificent Sinharaja Rainforest (4-5 hours drive), Sri Lanka's last viable area of primary tropical rainforest and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Afternoon

RAINFOREST TREK

Embark on an optional guided trek to explore the rich biodiversity of Sinharaja, discovering its unique flora and fauna with an expert naturalist.

03
Sinharaja Exploration
Full Day

WILDLIFE ENCOUNTERS

Full day exploring Sinharaja Rainforest with opportunities for bird watching, wildlife spotting, and discovering endemic flora species in their natural habitat. Keep an eye out for purple-faced langurs, Sri Lankan jungle fowl, and many of Sri Lanka's endemic bird species.

04
Journey to Ella
Morning

SCENIC JOURNEY

Depart for Ella after breakfast, embarking on a 4-5 hour journey through Sri Lanka's diverse landscapes from lowland rainforest to misty mountains.

Afternoon

MOUNTAIN RAILWAYS

Experience a spectacular train journey through lush tea plantations and picturesque landscapes from Nanu Oya to Ella, one of the world's most scenic rail routes.

05
Ella Adventures
Morning

ELLA ROCK HIKE

Early morning hike to Ella Rock for panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and peaks, perfect for spotting mountain birds and enjoying the cool mountain air.

Afternoon

ICONIC LANDMARKS

Visit the Nine Arch Bridge and explore Little Adam's Peak for more stunning views and nature photography opportunities. Keep your camera ready for the rich birdlife in this region.

06
Yala National Park
Morning

JOURNEY TO YALA

After breakfast, depart for Yala National Park (2-3 hours drive), home to one of the highest leopard densities in the world. Your expert guide will share insights about the park's ecosystem during the journey.

Afternoon

LEISURE TIME

Relax at our wilderness lodge, enjoy the pool, or take a nature walk around the property with opportunities to spot local wildlife near the accommodation.

07
Yala Safari
Morning

WILDLIFE SAFARI

Early morning safari at Yala National Park, searching for leopards, elephants, and diverse bird species in their natural habitat. Your experienced tracker will help spot and identify different species.

Afternoon

ECOSYSTEM EXPLORATION

Discover the park's diverse ecosystems and landscapes, from coastal wetlands to grasslands and forests. Learn about the intricate relationships between different species in this unique habitat.

08
Coastal Journey
Morning

MIRISSA BOUND

Depart for Mirissa after breakfast, a 3-4 hour scenic drive along the southern coast, watching the landscape transition from wilderness to coastal paradise.

Afternoon

BEACH RELAXATION

Check into our beachfront accommodation and unwind on Mirissa's beautiful beaches. Watch for sea birds and enjoy the coastal atmosphere.

09
Marine Life
Morning

WHALE WATCHING

Early morning whale watching expedition to spot blue whales, sperm whales, and dolphins in their natural habitat. Our marine expert will provide insights into these magnificent creatures.

Afternoon

COASTAL EXPLORATION

Explore local markets, enjoy fresh seafood, and watch the sunset from a beachside café while observing the coastal birdlife.

10
Colombo
Morning

CAPITAL BOUND

Head to Colombo in the morning (approximately 3 hours), watching the landscape transform from coastal scenery to urban sophistication as we approach Sri Lanka's vibrant capital.

Afternoon

CITY EXPLORATION

Enjoy a city tour through Colombo's highlights and experience the vibrant nightlife for your final evening. Visit the city's parks to spot urban wildlife and birds.

11
Departure
(Depending on flight schedule)
Morning

FINAL MORNING

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel while reflecting on your wildlife adventures. Depending on your flight schedule, there may be time for some last-minute shopping or sightseeing.

Departure

FAREWELL

Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport (3-4 hours drive) for your departure flight, taking with you unforgettable memories of Sri Lanka's incredible wildlife and natural wonders.
