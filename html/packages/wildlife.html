Day by Day Itinerary

Your wildlife journey through paradise

01
Negombo
Morning

AIRPORT PICKUP

Upon your arrival, we will pick you up from the airport and transport you to your accommodation, where you can unwind and prepare for the exciting journey ahead.

Afternoon

Rest & Relaxation

Settle into your comfortable hotel in Negombo, taking time to unwind and prepare for the exciting wildlife adventure ahead.

02
Wilpattu
Morning

FISHING VILLAGE

Start your day exploring the vibrant Negombo fishing village, witnessing the traditional fishing methods and experiencing the local way of life.

Afternoon

SAFARI

Journey to Wilpattu National Park (3-4 hours), followed by an evening safari to spot the park’s famous leopard population and other nocturnal wildlife.

03
Wilpattu National Park
Morning

WILDLIFE SAFARI

Embark on a morning safari through Wilpattu’s unique ecosystem, searching for sloth bears and elephants in their natural habitat.

Afternoon

CONTINUED EXPLORATION

Continue your wildlife adventure, focusing on the park’s diverse bird species and other fascinating creatures that call Wilpattu home.

04
Habarana
Morning

SCENIC JOURNEY

After breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to Habarana (3-4 hours), taking in the changing landscapes of Sri Lanka’s cultural triangle.

Afternoon

MINNERIYA SAFARI

Experience the famous elephant gatherings at Minneriya National Park, one of Asia’s most spectacular wildlife events.

05
Sigiriya
Morning

EARLY MORNING ADVENTURE

Early morning visit to Sigiriya Rock Fortress for a hike and wildlife spotting from the top. The magnificent viewpoint offers a perfect opportunity to observe various bird species and the surrounding wilderness.

Afternoon

CHOOSE YOUR PATH

Option 1: Trek up Pidurangala Rock for panoramic views and wildlife encounters.

Option 2: Explore Polonnaruwa Ancient City, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, for historical and wildlife experiences.

06
Udawalawe
Morning

SCENIC JOURNEY

Begin your journey to Udawalawe National Park (5-6 hours), traveling through Sri Lanka’s diverse landscapes.

Afternoon

LEISURE TIME

Settle into your accommodation near Udawalawe and enjoy the peaceful surroundings of the Sri Lankan wilderness.

07
Udawalawe National Park
Morning

ELEPHANT EXPERIENCE

Embark on a morning safari in Udawalawe, famous for its substantial elephant population and excellent viewing opportunities.

Afternoon

CONSERVATION INSIGHT (OPTIONAL)

Visit the Elephant Transit Home to witness conservation in action and learn about elephant rehabilitation efforts.

08
Yala
Morning

YALA

Next, we will journey from Udawalawe to Yala, ready to explore its diverse wildlife and stunning natural beauty in the heart of Sri Lanka’s wilderness.

Afternoon

RESORT RELAXATION

Unwind at your accommodation near Yala, preparing for tomorrow’s wildlife adventures.

09
Yala National Park
Morning

SAFARI

Experience an early morning safari in Yala, maximizing your chances of spotting the elusive Sri Lankan leopard.

Afternoon

CULTURAL CONNECTION (OPTIONAL)

Visit to the sacred Kataragama Temple, blending wildlife experiences with cultural exploration.

10
Farewell to Sri Lanka
Morning

FINAL MOMENTS

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast while reflecting on your wildlife adventures.

Afternoon

DEPARTURE

Begin your journey back to Katunayake International Airport (5-6 hours), carrying memories of Sri Lanka’s magnificent wildlife.
