Day by Day Itinerary

Your journey along the eastern paradise, carefully planned for an unforgettable experience

01
Negombo
Morning

AIRPORT ARRIVAL

Welcome to Sri Lanka! Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, our representative will meet and transfer you to your hotel in Negombo. Rest and refresh after your journey.

Afternoon

LOCAL EXPLORATION

Visit the bustling fish market and explore the charming Negombo beach. Experience the local fishing community.

02
Trincomalee
Morning

JOURNEY TO TRINCOMALEE

After breakfast, embark on a scenic journey to Trincomalee. Watch as the landscape transforms from urban to coastal scenery during the drive.

Afternoon

CULTURAL EXPLORATION

Visit the iconic Koneswaram Temple and explore Fort Frederick. Enjoy stunning views of the natural harbor and coastline.

03
Pigeon Island
Morning

SNORKELING ADVENTURE

Take a boat to Pigeon Island National Park for snorkeling. Discover vibrant coral reefs and tropical fish in crystal-clear waters.

Afternoon

BEACH RELAXATION

Relax on the beaches and enjoy the scenery.

04
Batticaloa
Morning

COASTAL JOURNEY

Drive south to Batticaloa, known for its historic lighthouse and beautiful lagoons. Check into your accommodation.

Afternoon

HISTORICAL SITES

Visit the Batticaloa Fort and take a leisurely walk along the scenic Kallady Bridge, where you can enjoy stunning views of the lagoon. Explore the historic Dutch Canal area.

05
Batticaloa Exploration
Morning

CYCLING TOUR

Enjoy a guided cycling tour through local villages and along the scenic lagoon. Visit traditional markets and temples.

Afternoon

BEACH TIME

Relax at Pasikuda Beach, known for its shallow waters and long stretches of white sand.

06
Arugam Bay
Morning

JOURNEY SOUTH

Drive to Arugam Bay, Sri Lanka's surfing paradise. Check into your beachfront accommodation and get acquainted with the laid-back surfing atmosphere.

Afternoon

SURF AND BEACHES

Spend the day surfing or relaxing on the famous beaches of Arugam Bay

07
Arugam Bay Activities
Morning

SURF AND EXPLORE

Take an optional introductory surfing lesson or explore nearby beaches. Visit the famous Elephant Rock and Whiskey Point surf spots.

Afternoon

COASTAL LIFE

Experience the vibrant beach culture and nightlife of Arugam Bay.

08
Kumana National Park
Morning

WILDLIFE SAFARI

Early morning departure to Kumana National Park. Enjoy a safari experience spotting diverse wildlife and bird species.

Evening

CAMPING EXPERIENCE

Set up camp in the park. Enjoy dinner under the stars and experience the sounds of nature at night.

09
Journey to Ella
Morning

HILL COUNTRY BOUND

Depart for Ella, watching the landscape transform from coastal plains to misty mountains. Check into your mountain view accommodation.

Afternoon

MOUNTAIN VIEWS

Visit a tea plantation and factory. Learn about Ceylon tea production while enjoying mountain vistas.

10
Colombo
Morning

JOURNEY TO COLOMBO

After breakfast, begin your scenic journey to Colombo. Watch as the landscape transforms from misty mountains to urban cityscape.

Afternoon

CITY EXPLORATION

Visit Independence Square and Galle Face Green. Enjoy shopping at local markets and a farewell dinner at a selected restaurant.

11
Departure
(Depending on flight schedule)
Morning

FINAL MORNING

Free time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing in Colombo, depending on your flight schedule.

Departure

FAREWELL

Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for your departure flight, concluding your Sri Lankan east coast adventure.
