Day by Day Itinerary

Your cycling and camping journey through Sri Lanka, carefully planned for an unforgettable adventure

01
Negombo
Morning

AIRPORT PICKUP

Upon your arrival, we will pick you up from the airport and transport you to your accommodation, where you can unwind and prepare for the exciting journey ahead.

Afternoon

FISHING VILLAGE

Immerse yourself in the vibrant coastal life of Negombo as we guide you through the bustling local fish market. Later, unwind with a leisurely stroll along the golden sands of Negombo beach, taking in your first Sri Lankan sunset over the Indian Ocean.

02
Wilpattu National Park
Morning

JOURNEY TO THE WILDERNESS

After breakfast, we'll embark on an exciting journey to Wilpattu National Park, Sri Lanka's largest wildlife sanctuary. As we travel, watch the landscape transform from coastal scenes to wild jungle terrain, setting the stage for your upcoming adventure.

Afternoon

SAFARI AND STARLIT CAMPING

Experience the thrill of setting up camp in the heart of Wilpattu National Park. As the day cools, we'll embark on an evening safari or bird watching excursion. As night falls, enjoy dinner under a canopy of stars, surrounded by the sounds of the wilderness.

03
Polonnaruwa
Morning

JOURNEY TO ANCIENT KINGDOM

After breaking camp, we'll journey from the wilderness of Wilpattu to the historic city of Polonnaruwa, Sri Lanka's medieval capital. The transition from natural landscapes to ancient civilization sets the stage for an afternoon of archaeological exploration.

Afternoon

CYCLING THROUGH HISTORY

Mount your bicycle for an unforgettable exploration of Polonnaruwa's UNESCO World Heritage site. Pedal through history as you visit the magnificent Polonnaruwa Vatadage, marvel at the serene Buddha statues of Gal Vihara, and witness the engineering marvel of Parakrama Samudra, an ancient irrigation tank.

04
Trincomalee
Morning

COASTAL JOURNEY

Head east to the coastal paradise of Trincomalee, where crystal-clear waters and pristine beaches await. The scenic drive takes us through changing landscapes, from the ancient ruins to the stunning eastern coastline.

Afternoon

BEACH AND CULTURE

Unwind on Trincomalee's pristine beaches before embarking on a cultural exploration. Visit the magnificent Koneswaram Temple, perched dramatically on Swami Rock, and explore the historic Fort Frederick, where colonial history meets ancient traditions. The evening light casts a golden glow over the bay, perfect for photography and reflection.

05
Pigeon Island
Full Day

UNDERWATER ADVENTURE

A short boat ride takes you to the stunning Pigeon Island National Park, one of Sri Lanka's premier marine sanctuaries. Spend your day snorkeling or diving, discovering vibrant coral gardens and encountering diverse marine life.

06
Habarana & Sigiriya
Morning

CULTURAL HEARTLAND

Bid farewell to the coast as we journey inland to Habarana. The landscape transforms dramatically as we approach the heart of Sri Lanka's ancient civilization.

Afternoon

LION ROCK EXPLORATION

Experience the majestic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, first by bicycle through its surrounding gardens and moats, then on foot as you ascend this architectural marvel. The climb rewards you with spectacular views of the surrounding landscape and ancient water gardens.

07
Colombo
Morning

CAPITAL BOUND

We begin our journey to Colombo, Sri Lanka's vibrant capital city. Watch as rural landscapes gradually give way to the bustling energy of urban life.

Afternoon

CITY EXPLORATION

Choose your adventure: explore the city's colonial heritage and modern developments on a thrilling tuk-tuk tour, or opt for the unique "Colombo by Jeep" experience. Visit iconic landmarks including the historic Independence Square, the bustling Galle Face Green waterfront, and the vibrant Pettah Market.

08
Colombo Deep Dive
Day Activities

URBAN CYCLING ADVENTURE

Experience Colombo from a new perspective as we cycle through its diverse neighborhoods. Visit the National Museum to delve into Sri Lanka's rich history, and explore the serene Gangaramaya Temple.

Evening

FAREWELL DINNER

Conclude your adventure with an unforgettable dining experience at "The Blue Orbit" rooftop restaurant. Enjoy panoramic views of the illuminated city while savoring excellent cuisine and reflecting on your journey through Sri Lanka.

09
Departure
(Depending on flight schedule)
Morning

FINAL MOMENTS

Depending on your flight schedule, take advantage of your remaining time to pick up last-minute souvenirs from local markets or enjoy a final stroll through the city's charming streets.

Afternoon

AIRPORT

We'll ensure a smooth transfer to Katunayake International Airport for your departure flight, bidding farewell to the island paradise that has been your home for the past nine days, filled with unforgettable cycling adventures and camping experiences.
